Lasanga Recipe

A lasagna is a type of italian dish which consists of mutliple layers of cooked mince beef mixed with tomato sauce, bechamel sauce and mozzarela cheese with layers of lasangna sheets.
This recipe requires you to dice onion and garlic and reduce with olive oil and mix tomato paste with tomato sauce and reduce until thick. Then make bechamel sauce and layer it

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • garlic
  • Tomato Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Lasangna sheets
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook onion with olive oil until translucent
  2. Throw in minced garlic and cook for 5 minutes
  3. Pour in tomato paste and fry it with olive oil
  4. Pour in tomato sauce
  5. Make bechamel in a new pot by heating butter and flour into a roux
  6. Layer bechamel and then tomato sauce then lasangna sheets
